Jaguar Racing

Who We Are

Jag Racing, a student-led initiative, is gearing up for participation in the 2024 Baja SAE® competition. Our primary objective is to provide students with the opportunity to engage in a collaborative engineering environment. Beyond the race track, we are committed to fostering an environment where students can apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges. By participating in the competition, students gain invaluable hands-on experience in vehicle design, fabrication, and problem-solving. Jag Racing is dedicated to empowering students to thrive in an engineering setting, preparing them for future endeavors and challenges in the field.
